### Projects

**[🦄 Global Unicorn Companies Analysis](https://github.com/nupoork8/Global-Unicorn-Companies-Analysis)**
`SQL` `Python` `Tableau`

1,074 startups. One question: what actually separates the ones that make it?
Mapped funding patterns, time-to-unicorn trends, and capital efficiency across 48 countries.
The finding that stuck: Canva turned every $1 raised into $70 of valuation. Stripe, $47. Most companies don't come close.
→ 3 Tableau dashboards · 8 SQL queries · full EDA pipeline

---

**[🛍️ Shopify Trending Product Analysis](https://github.com/nupoork8/Shopify-Trending-Product-Analysis)**
`T-SQL` `Python` `Pearson Correlation`

Are viral products actually worth chasing? I ran the numbers.
Viral-tier products (95+ trend score) move 849K units but earn $32/unit.
The "Hot" tier earns 3× more per sale — with a fraction of the noise.
Pearson r = -0.060. Trend score and profitability are basically unrelated.
→ Composite opportunity score model · 261 pre-peak products identified
